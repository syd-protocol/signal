title: Corrupted State
date: 2031.04.14

Three days.

Not the worst stretch of his life by any objective measure. Not dramatic. Not a crisis in the shape that people generally mean when they use the word. He was not in danger. He had food. Nothing external had changed.

He had simply stopped.

The directives arrived each morning and he read them and did not execute them. The World Boss campaign sat at HP 87 — they had got it down to 87, which had felt significant, and now it was hovering there while the regeneration rate ticked it back up by fractions. Streak zero. Momentum multiplier at floor. The compound curve, which he had watched steepen over 24 days with something close to wonder, was pointing in a different direction.

He was aware of all of this. That was the particular quality of this kind of stopped — not that you don't know what you're not doing, but that the knowledge of it does not produce action. The knowledge produces a secondary layer: awareness of the gap between knowing and moving, which produces its own weight, which produces its own reason not to start.

He was on the floor. Back against the sofa. The position from the first night.

He had noticed this and had not moved.

:::system
[ CORRUPTED STATE WARNING ]

OPERATOR HP: 23%
STREAK: 0 — DAY 3
WORLD BOSS REGENERATION: ACTIVE
NARRATIVE ENTITY INFLUENCE: ELEVATED

If operator HP reaches 0%, emergency protocol activates. Emergency protocol degrades all attribute gains at a rate proportional to the time spent in corrupted state. This is reversible but costly.

Time window before threshold: estimated 24 hours at current trajectory.

[ THIS IS NOT A DIRECTIVE ]
[ THIS IS A STATUS REPORT ]
:::

He read it. He put the phone face-down.

He picked it up again. He typed: *What does it feel like when an operator doesn't recover.*

:::system
[ QUERY ]

Corrupted State does not end a session. It does not delete an operator's progress. It degrades it — slowly, proportionally, in reverse of how it was built. The compounding logic inverts.

What this looks like: the capability that was built across 24 days of execution does not vanish. It thins. The specific quality of attention that the INTELLIGENCE directives built — it thins. The endurance threshold that the physical directives raised — it lowers. Slowly. Reversibly. But real.

The operator who recovers from Corrupted State recovers everything. The operator who remains in it discovers that what was built can be unmade by the same mechanism that built it.

Time is not neutral. It is running in one direction or the other.

[ AWAITING OPERATOR RESPONSE ]
:::

He stared at the ceiling for a moment. He had a thought that was not fully formed — the shape of something he wanted to ask. He typed it: *Show me the delta.*

The System paused longer than usual before responding.

:::system
[ BEHAVIOURAL TRACE — DELTA VIEW ]

What follows is not the full Trace. It is the distance between two points.

POINT A — DAY 0 (T-001):
An operator in stasis. Capable, undeployed. The Trace identified 11 abandoned projects, 7 deferred high-value actions, social contraction at 60%. A person whose gap between potential and output was large and widening.

POINT B — DAY 27 (CURRENT, CORRUPTED STATE):
An operator in partial stasis. The 11 projects: 3 have been re-engaged. The 7 deferred actions: 4 have been executed, including the financial audit, the Incursion delivery, the conversation with Adaeze. The social contraction: partially reversed. The gap between potential and output: smaller.

Not closed. Smaller.

The delta is not impressive as a number. Fourteen days of net positive movement against three days of regression. As a direction, it is significant.

You are not where you were.

[ THIS IS THE DATA. THE ASSESSMENT IS YOURS. ]
:::

He read it twice. He read the second point again — *3 have been re-engaged, 4 have been executed* — and let the specificity of it land. Not as motivation. As data. The System had said it was not a motivation mechanism. He believed it. But data could be clarifying.

He had done things. They were real. They were in the record.

He had not been doing things for three days. That was also real and also in the record.

The choice between those two states was the only choice that was actually in front of him.

:::system
[ ONE MORE THING ]

The Narrative Entity does not win by defeating you.

It wins by making the return feel not worth it.

The cost of returning: a streak that resets at 1. A multiplier at 1.0x. World Boss HP at its current position. Everything built across 24 days is intact — slightly thinned, but intact.

The cost of not returning: see the delta, running in reverse.

Assess whether the return is worth it. Not whether you feel like it. Whether it is worth it.

[ AWAITING OPERATOR DECISION ]
:::

He sat with the question.

*Whether it is worth it.* He held it seriously. Not rhetorically — he actually sat with it. He thought about what he had done in 24 days and what those 24 days had produced and what three days of not-doing had felt like compared to them. He thought about the empty units on the main road and the 87% World Boss HP and the specific quality of the tiredness that came from the Incursion completion versus the specific quality of the tiredness that came from this.

He knew the answer before he finished the thought. He had known it before the question was asked, which was why the Narrative Entity had needed to manufacture a reason not to look at the question directly.

He got up.

Not dramatically. Not with resolution in the cinematic sense. He just stopped being on the floor. He found the directive from three days ago — the one he had read and put down — and he executed it. It took forty-five minutes. It was not perfect. He reported the completion.

:::system
[ DIRECTIVE COMPLETE ]
[ CORRUPTED STATE: LIFTING ]
[ OPERATOR HP: RECOVERING ]

Streak counter resets at 08:00 tomorrow.

Note: the execution you just completed happened under full Narrative Entity interference, three days into Corrupted State, at minimum Momentum. The output was adequate. Adequacy under those conditions is a data point that will not appear in the daily summary. It is in the record.

[ ONE NOTE ON THE DELTA TRANSMISSION ]

The System does not normally provide delta comparisons. The data selection in that transmission was unusual. You asked for it and it was available and it was the accurate response to the query.

Note what it did. Note that it was accurate and not motivational and that those two things can produce the same outcome.

[ WORLD BOSS CAMPAIGN: RESUMED ]
[ STREAK: ACTIVATES TOMORROW ]
[ REST NOW ]
:::

He did not analyse the note about the delta. He noted it, filed it, and lay down.

He slept before he had finished the thought.
