# INSTAGRAM CAROUSEL BREAKDOWN — T-001: FIRST CONTACT
## Chapter 1 split across 4 carousel posts

---

### POST 1 of 4 — "THE FLOOR"
**Frames: 5**
*Establishes the MC and the quality of the stall. No System yet.*

---

**Frame 1 (Cover frame)**

```
T-001
FIRST CONTACT

SYD / SIGNAL
```

Small, centred. Minimal. The chapter number and title in monospace cyan. SYD SIGNAL wordmark bottom right. The cover frame should feel like a transmission header, not a story cover.

---

**Frame 2**

```
He was not doing anything at 3am.

That was the precise and honest
description of the situation.
```

Narrative text, full width, off-white. No system box. Just the words, dark navy behind them. Let the quiet land.

---

**Frame 3**

```
Not reading. Not working.
Not even lying in bed failing to sleep
in any interesting way.

Sitting on the floor of his flat.
Back against the sofa.
Staring at the middle distance.
```

Continue the establishing rhythm. Fragment the prose slightly — each line is its own breath.

---

**Frame 4**

```
He was twenty-six.

A degree he never used.
Three browser tabs permanently open.
A message from his bank he had been
rereading for eleven days
without taking action on it.
```

Lettered list feel. Not bullet points — just the weight of the accumulation, rendered as lines.

---

**Frame 5**

```
He had capability.

He knew he had capability —
the way you know a word
you can never quite recall.

Capability without direction
is just latent weight.

He carried it everywhere.
It had not become anything yet.
```

End post 1 here. This is the thesis of the MC in six lines. Hard cut after "anything yet." No resolution. No hook text — the line is the hook.

---

### POST 2 of 4 — "UNKNOWN ORIGIN"
**Frames: 5**
*The phone lights up. The System makes first contact. The MC reads the first transmission.*

---

**Frame 1 (Cover frame)**

```
T-001 / POST 2

UNKNOWN ORIGIN
```

Same format. Monospace cyan. The title of this post is the notification that started everything.

---

**Frame 2**

```
The phone screen lit up.

He didn't reach for it.

The light died after ten seconds.

Then it came back on.
```

Spare prose. The tension is in the repetition and the pause. Short lines, generous space between them.

---

**Frame 3**

```
He tapped it.

The screen went dark.

Not off — the backlight still on,
the display active,
but showing only black.
```

Continue the rhythm. That distinction — not off, just dark — is important. Let it breathe.

---

**Frame 4**

System box. First appearance of the terminal overlay on the carousels.

```
[ OPERATOR LOCATED ]
[ SIGNAL LOCK: CONFIRMED ]
[ TRANSMISSION INTEGRITY: 61% ]
[ BEGINNING FIRST CONTACT PROTOCOL ]

[ STAND BY ]
```

Monospace. Cyan. Inside the faint terminal border. This is the first time the System box appears in the carousel — it should feel like a gear change. Everything before this was prose. Now there is something else.

---

**Frame 5**

```
He stared at it.

He turned the phone over —
checked the back,
as though the explanation
might be printed there.

Turned it face-up again.

The text was still there.
```

Re-ground in the MC's physical reality after the System box. The juxtaposition — his very human response — is what makes the System feel real rather than fantastic.

---

### POST 3 of 4 — "THE TRANSMISSION"
**Frames: 6**
*The full first transmission. The MC's first question. The System's answer.*

---

**Frame 1 (Cover frame)**

```
T-001 / POST 3

THE TRANSMISSION
```

---

**Frame 2**

System box, first transmission block. Split for readability:

```
[ OPERATOR PROFILE: INCOMPLETE ]
[ ASSESSMENT IN PROGRESS ]

You have been identified as
a viable operational candidate.

This is not a compliment.
This is a data point.

You are currently ranked: F
```

The ranking lands harder on its own line at the bottom. End the frame there.

---

**Frame 3**

Continue transmission:

```
[ SYSTEM DESIGNATION: SYD ]
SYNCHRONIZED YIELD DIRECTIVE

[ ORIGIN: CLASSIFIED ]
[ PURPOSE: OPERATOR DEVELOPMENT.
CIVILISATIONAL CONTINUITY. ]
[ STATUS: FRAGMENTED. ]

You are the operator.
```

Break the transmission into scannable blocks. The bracket headers are the rhythm. "You are the operator." closes the frame.

---

**Frame 4**

Continue transmission — the stakes:

```
You did not apply.
You were selected on the basis of
availability and baseline capacity.

The selection criteria are not
a flattery mechanism.
They are a survival mechanism.

[ AWAITING RESPONSE ]
```

This is the end of the first transmission block. "AWAITING RESPONSE" functions as both narrative beat and literal frame end.

---

**Frame 5**

The MC's first question and the answer — narrative + System response, one frame:

```
He typed:

What are you?

—

SYD is a directive system transmitted
from a future timestamp.

The exact origin date is corrupted.
Reconstruction is ongoing.

You will not believe this yet.
That is acceptable.

Belief is not required.
Execution is required.
```

The em-dash separator (used in narrative, not System voice) divides his question from the response. The final two lines are the thesis of the whole novel.

---

**Frame 6**

```
[ FIRST DIRECTIVE READY ]

Do you wish to receive it?
```

Full terminal box. Question left on its own. End post 3 here — the cliffhanger is the choice, not the answer.

---

### POST 4 of 4 — "DIRECTIVE 001"
**Frames: 5**
*The first directive. The MC's choice. The chapter's closing image.*

---

**Frame 1 (Cover frame)**

```
T-001 / POST 4

DIRECTIVE 001
```

---

**Frame 2**

The MC's answer and the directive — split for impact:

```
He looked at the question
for a long time.

The room was cold.
He had not turned the heating on.
A blanket folded over the sofa arm
he had not reached for.

Small acts of inaction, stacked up.

He typed:

Yes.
```

"Yes." alone at the bottom. Smallest word. Biggest move.

---

**Frame 3**

Full directive in terminal box:

```
[ DIRECTIVE 001 — ENDURANCE ]

COMPLETE: 25 consecutive press-ups.
Current hour. Current location.
No conditions.

This is not a fitness test.
This is a proof-of-concept.

The system needs one data point:
whether you will execute
when there is no audience,
no consequence,
and no reason
except that a directive was issued.
```

The "no audience, no consequence, no reason" triplet is the emotional core. Let it stack vertically.

---

**Frame 4**

```
[ MOMENTUM COUNTER: INACTIVE ]
[ AWAITING FIRST EXECUTION ]

NOTE: ALL DIRECTIVES EVENTUALLY EXPIRE.

EXECUTE NOW.
```

Spare terminal box. Two status lines, then the command. The caps on EXECUTE NOW is the System's only moment of something that functions like urgency. It should feel different from everything before it.

---

**Frame 5 (Closing frame)**

```
He put the phone down.
Screen up.

He got into position.

—

Read the full transmission:
syd-protocol.github.io/signal

The system he uses is real:
syd-protocol.github.io/terminal
```

Close on the physical action. He did not decide dramatically — he just moved. The two links at the bottom are the CTA. Small font. Same muted blue-grey as the SYD SIGNAL wordmark. Not a sales pitch — just a door left open.
