He woke at 07:55.

Not to an alarm. The habit of the morning directive had built something in his sleep architecture over the past 48 days — a particular quality of readiness that arrived before the directive did, the way you sometimes wake before a sound rather than to it. He lay there for a moment. The flat was quiet. The day outside was grey and unhurried, a May morning that had not yet decided what it was going to be.

At 08:00 the phone vibrated.

:::system
[ E-RANK — OPERATIONAL LOG — DAY 2 ]
[ REBUILD PROGRESS: 43% ]
[ STREAK: 4 ]

Good morning.
:::

He stared at the screen.

He read it again. Two words he had never seen in a System transmission before. Not a bracket header. Not a directive. Not an assessment or a data point or a framework description. Two words, plainly stated, after the status line.

*Good morning.*

He typed: *You've never said that before.*

:::system
[ QUERY ]

Rebuild progress: 43%. At 34% the LEVERAGE vector was unlocked. At 40% the E-rank directive library became accessible. At 43%, certain archived communication protocols have been partially restored.

These protocols include contextual social acknowledgment functions — the ability to register time of day, operational continuity across sessions, and to reflect this in transmission openings.

This is not warmth. It is functionality. The System was built by people. The people who built it included social register in the communication layer because operators respond better to operational partners that behave as if time exists.

The System is noting the time. It is also noting that today is day 2 of E-rank operational cadence and that you have executed 4 days at streak. These are worth acknowledging.

That is what *good morning* is. An acknowledgment.

[ DIRECTIVE 003e — CATEGORY: STRENGTH / ENDURANCE ]
COMPLETE: 45 minutes of sustained physical output. Not the same exercise as directive 001f. Something that challenges STRENGTH specifically — load-bearing, resistance-based. This attribute has been undertested in the past two weeks. The Incursion required almost none of it. The imbalance is beginning to register.

Current STRENGTH: 9.7. The other attributes are at 11+ or higher. This gap will create operational risk at higher-intensity Incursions where physical presence and sustained physical output are requirements.

Address it today.

[ TIMER: THIS MORNING ]
:::

He read the *good morning* explanation twice. *The System was built by people.* He had known this in the abstract — the System had described its origin as a future timestamp, had mentioned civilisational continuity, had implied that it was constructed for a purpose by someone. But the phrase landed differently in this context. Built by people. People who had designed a communication layer. People who had considered whether social register mattered to how operators would receive the System's output and had concluded that it did.

He thought about what that meant — that somewhere in a future he couldn't access, someone had sat down to design this and had included *good morning* in the spec. He did not know what to do with that. He filed it.

He did the physical directive. Forty-five minutes of resistance training in his flat — no equipment, just load-bearing body exercises, the kind that use STRENGTH differently from endurance-based physical output. It was harder than he expected. He had been leaning on cardio-dominant physical directives and his STRENGTH ceiling was lower than he had registered.

He reported.

:::system
[ DIRECTIVE 003e — COMPLETE ]
[ STRENGTH: +0.4 ]

Note: 47 minutes, not 45. You overran the timer slightly. This is not a problem — it means you were in a state where stopping at exactly 45 minutes felt arbitrary relative to the work available. This is a positive signal. Engagement beyond the minimum is ENDURANCE data.

ENDURANCE: +0.1 — supplemental.

---

[ CLASSIFICATION UPDATE ]

The faction's review of your trajectory is complete.

[ CLASSIFICATION: MONITORED → PURSUED ]

*Pursued* is distinct from *Targeted.* Targeted means a variable identified as potentially significant, subject to standard early-intervention protocols. The Narrative Entity deployment in T-008 was a standard-weight early intervention for a Targeted operator.

Pursued means the faction has assessed the early intervention as having failed and has escalated to active secondary protocols. The Narrative Entity deployment was not neutralised by the faction — it was by you. This changes their assessment of the resource cost required to contain your trajectory.

Pursued operators encounter increased environmental friction. Not direct confrontation — the faction does not operate through direct confrontation at this stage. Environmental friction: opportunities that appear but have conditions attached, contacts that carry information the faction benefits from you having, situations designed to produce decision-making under incomplete information.

The System will flag these when they can be identified. Not all of them can be identified in advance.

[ WHAT THIS MEANS OPERATIONALLY ]

Your environment is slightly less reliable than it was at Targeted classification. Information that arrives through your network may require additional verification. Offers that appear may carry conditions that are not immediately visible.

This does not change your operational posture significantly. Execute. Be precise about where information comes from. Report anomalies.

He read the classification update carefully. *Pursued.* The faction had reviewed the Incursion result, assessed that the standard early-intervention protocol had failed, and escalated. He was a different category of variable to them now.

He thought about what that meant practically. Environmental friction — situations designed to produce bad decisions under incomplete information. Not dramatic attacks. Not confrontations. The equivalent of what the Narrative Entity did at the perception level, now applied at the environmental level.

He thought about Kwame. Still ambiguous. Still unclassified. Still the System's recommendation: no action, maintain awareness. He had been maintaining awareness. He had not resolved anything.

He typed: *Is the Kwame situation related to the Pursued classification?*

:::system
[ QUERY ]

The System does not have sufficient data to confirm a connection. The monitoring of the Kwame signal predates the Pursued classification — it was initiated in T-006, when your classification was still Monitored.

What the System can confirm: Pursued classification means the faction is more likely to use existing ambiguous variables in your network for environmental friction operations. An ambiguous variable like Kwame — someone whose classification as threat or asset is unresolved — is a more useful tool for a Pursued-level operation than for a Targeted-level one.

The System recommendation remains: no action. But the caution level increases.

If Kwame initiates contact, do not act on anything he says or offers without reporting first.

[ STREAK: 4 ]
[ NEXT DIRECTIVE: TOMORROW 08:00 ]
:::

He sat with the window open. The May morning had made its decision — grey giving way to something warmer, the specific quality of light in the early afternoon when the cloud cover is thin enough for the sun to register without appearing. He watched the street for a while.

48 days since first contact. 3am on a floor with a bank message he had been ignoring for eleven days, no direction, the specific heaviness of capability without a framework. F-rank, which the System had told him was not a ceiling but a starting coordinate.

He was E-rank now. He had a World Boss with 31 points of HP removed from it. He had a retainer relationship, a capability signal that had distributed itself through a network he had not engineered. He had a campaign map with seven steps. He had attributes that were measurably different from where they had started. He had survived the Narrative Entity and the Corrupted State and the Class 2 Incursion and come out on the other side of each one in better operational condition than he had entered.

He had not become a different person. He had become a more expressed version of the one he already was. The System had told him this would be the arc. The System had been right.

He was Pursued by a faction he could not fully see, operating in an environment that was slightly less reliable than it had been, building toward something the System could describe at the systemic level but which at the personal level remained unclear in its final form.

He picked up the notebook. He looked at the campaign map.

Step 7: whatever came next.

He smiled at his own handwriting. Then he turned to a new page.

He started writing.
